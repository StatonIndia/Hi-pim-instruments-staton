About Us
STATON, s.r.o. is a machinery company in northern Slovakia, concentrating on the development and construction of PVD coating equipment, production and reconditioning of tools, and improving tool quality through superior PVD coatings. Since 1998, we have applied the newest knowledge in nanotechnology and plasma technology to deliver complete coating solutions worldwide.

The Science

PRECISION DEFINES
PERFORMANCE.

The technologies that make every coating a work of precision engineering.

01
PVD
Physical Vapour Deposition - advanced thin-film coating technology delivering superior wear resistance, hardness, and adhesion at atomic precision.
02
fsARC® Cathode
Our own proprietary arc source, developed in-house, providing high ionization and excellent adhesion for hard, high-performance coatings.
03
HIPIMS & Sputtering
Magnetron sputtering in HIPIMS and DC modes for smooth surfaces, material variability, and precise control of coating thickness.
04
Nano & Plasma
The newest scientific knowledge in nanotechnology and plasma technology, applied to develop novel coatings that increase component quality and performance.
