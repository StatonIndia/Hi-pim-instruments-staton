fsARC™ vs Conventional Coating

Optimal Homogeneity

Standard Arc Coating

Conventional arc coating processes often produce a rough, uneven surface riddled with micro-droplets. This texture creates significantly higher friction during heavy-duty machining, leading to accelerated wear and a drastically shorter tool lifespan compared to filtered systems.

fsARC™ Coating

STATON's patented fsARC™ technology delivers an exceptionally smooth coating surface with a minimal droplet content. By eliminating macro-particles at the source, it minimizes operational friction and maximizes machining efficiency, extending tool life by up to five times.

The ultimate multi-arc PVD system featuring 8 cathode positions, planetary rotation, and Staton's proprietary fsARC™ filtration for unmatched coating uniformity and adhesion.

Engineered for high-volume production environments, the OCTOARC delivers exceptional throughput without compromising on quality. Magnetron Series

OCTOMAG

Advanced magnetron sputtering platform with unbalanced field design, enabling high-ionization plasma for ultra-dense, low-stress decorative and functional coatings.

Ideal for decorative and functional finishes, the OCTOMAG produces smooth, dense coatings with outstanding adhesion and colour consistency. Its precise plasma control makes it well suited for applications where surface quality and aesthetic uniformity are paramount, from architectural hardware to precision components.
