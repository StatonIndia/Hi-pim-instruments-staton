fsARC™ vs Conventional Coating

Eliminate the droplets. Maximize performance.

Standard Arc Coating

Conventional arc coating processes often produce a rough, uneven surface riddled with micro-droplets. This texture creates significantly higher friction during heavy-duty machining, leading to accelerated wear and a drastically shorter tool lifespan compared to filtered systems.

fsARC™ Coating

Staton's proprietary fsARC™ technology delivers a flawless, droplet-free surface that is exceptionally smooth. By eliminating macro-particles at the source, it minimizes operational friction and maximizes machining efficiency, extending tool life by up to five times.

Tool Process Workflow

From used tool to high-performance coating - a precision-driven restoration and enhancement process.

Stripping

Removal of existing coating layers through controlled processes, restoring the tool to its base material while preserving structural integrity and dimensional accuracy.

Sharpening

Precision regrinding and edge restoration ensure optimal cutting geometry, improving performance, consistency, and machining efficiency.

Cleaning

Advanced cleaning processes eliminate contaminants, oils, and residues, preparing the surface for uniform coating adhesion in controlled environments.

PVD Coating

Application of high-performance PVD coating within vacuum-controlled chambers, delivering a uniform, wear-resistant surface with superior adhesion and durability.
